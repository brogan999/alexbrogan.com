    Agency is your ability to shape circumstances

    Agency is your ability to shape circumstances instead of being shaped by them. Most people live their entire lives as spectators. They watch others build companies, create movements, and change history. They attribute success to luck, timing, or privilege. This is a comforting illusion that protects their ego. The truth is simpler and harder to accept: agency is a choice. Every billionaire, world-changer, and history-maker made this choice. They decided they wouldn't accept the world as given. They would bend reality instead. This isn't mystical — it's mechanical. You either believe your actions matter or you don't. ...
